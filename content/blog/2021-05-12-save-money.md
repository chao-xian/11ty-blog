[Simon Hughesdon](https://twitter.com/sihugh) is thrifty and gave me some sage advice. The gist is...

1. spend less than you earn
2. minimise your outgoings (so you spend even less)
3. build up an emergency fund and pay off your debt
4. save/invest the rest in some way

Simple!
